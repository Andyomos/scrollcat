A Marketer Who Learned to Build. A Builder Who Came Back.

Anderson's background is an unusual combination, and it's exactly why ScrollCat works. He trained in Business & Marketing (Humber College) — so he understands brand, story, and what makes people actually click. Then he trained as a Cybersecurity Specialist (CDI College) — A+, Network+, Security+, CySA+, PenTest+, Linux, networking. So he understands systems, and how they break.

Marketing tells you what to build. Security tells you how to build it so it holds. Put them in one person and you get someone who can take a meme cat and wire it to a real, audited, on-chain economy — and then explain it in a sentence you'll remember.

For a while, life got loud. A family to feed, bills that don't wait, and the building took a back seat. The spark dimmed. Then it came back — and ScrollCat is what that comeback looks like: not a hobby, a shipped product.
